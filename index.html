Our professional team of cleaners and maintenance personnel are dedicated to providing high-quality services to keep your home or business clean, organized, and well-maintained. We understand that your time is valuable, which is why we offer flexible scheduling options to fit your busy lifestyle. Our team is available for one-time cleanings, weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly services, and can even create a customized cleaning plan to fit your specific needs.

Our house workers are trained and equipped ensuring that every inch of your space is left spotless. From dusting and washing to deep-cleaning bathrooms and kitchens, our team will leave your home or office looking and feeling fresh and inviting. In addition to our cleaning services, we also offer maintenance services to keep your space in top condition. From changing light bulbs to repairing leaky faucets, our team is equipped to handle any task to ensure your space is safe and functional. Located in Kigali, Rwanda, we are committed to providing exceptional customer service and making sure every client is completely satisfied with our work.
